# Streamer facts

Streamer-authored notes about *you* (the streamer), the channel, and any
recurring context the AI should know when interpreting chat. Five
generative features read this file: engaging-subjects extraction,
talking-points, thread recaps, subject talking-points, and
question answer-angles. A good facts file stops the AI from
flagging real channel jokes / arcs / inside terms as hallucinations.

How to use this template:
1. Click **Download default** in Settings → Prompts → Channel facts.
2. Fill in the bracketed sections below for your own channel.
3. Click **Upload .md file** to push it back, or paste into the editor.

Lines starting with `#` are headings; everything else is prose. Keep
it short — a few hundred words is plenty. A few thousand will start
eating your context budget on every refresh.

## About the streamer

(Name you go by on stream, real name if you want the AI to use it,
pronouns, region/timezone, how long you've been streaming, what kind
of content you usually do, day job if relevant.)

## What the channel is

(Primary game(s) or content type, typical schedule, average concurrent
viewer count, sponsorships chat may reference. Helps the AI know
whether "patch dropped" means the game you stream or something else.)

## Recurring bits / inside jokes

(Things chat references regularly that an outside AI wouldn't
recognise: pet names, running jokes, lore from past streams, emotes
with non-obvious meaning, real people who keep coming up. Each entry
should make it OBVIOUS that the thing is real and not a
hallucination to flag.)

## Current series / arcs

(Games or topics you're currently in the middle of: "doing a no-hit
run of X", "playthrough series of Y", "weekly Z night". Helps the
AI distinguish a one-off mention from an ongoing arc. Update this
when your focus shifts.)

## Known sensitive topics for this channel

(Anything beyond the default religion / politics / controversy filter
that you want treated as sensitive on YOUR channel specifically.
Conversely: if your channel's whole content IS political commentary
or rage-game frustration, note it here so the AI doesn't filter the
actual subject matter. Leave blank if the default filter is enough.)
