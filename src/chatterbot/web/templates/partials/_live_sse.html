{# Live-chat SSE driver. Chat-window flow: oldest rows at the top of the container, newest at the bottom (matches every other chat client the streamer uses). The server's SSE payload is the live partial — already newest-first — which we reverse to append in chronological order. New rows always land at the bottom; the container auto-scrolls to follow them when the streamer is already pinned to the bottom, but does NOT yank them out of reading position when they've scrolled up to read history. Initial render is server-side (HTMX one-shot for the sidebar, the page template itself for /live). The SSE stream takes over from there. Idle cost: ~0 bytes/s once the heartbeat lull settles in. Compare to the old poll: 30 full template renders per minute per page open. #}
